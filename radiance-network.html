Radiance Network

How our youth connects, shines, and serves alongside the wider NCCF community and partner youth ministries.

"You are the light of the world. A town built on a hill cannot be hidden."

Matthew 5:14

What is the Radiance Network?

Radiance Network is how NCCF Anointed Reigning Mighty Youth stays connected — sharing resources, prayer, and joint activities with other youth chapters, so that light multiplies rather than stays in one place.

Fellowship

Inter-Chapter Fellowship

Joint worship nights and fellowship events with partner NCCF youth chapters throughout the year.

Resources

Shared Resources

Bible study materials, worship sets, and event templates shared across the network to help every chapter grow.

Mission

Joint Outreach

Combined outreach missions that reach further than any single youth group could on its own.
